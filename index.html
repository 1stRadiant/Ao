About Us

Angels Outreach Community Interest Company is a community-led social enterprise, created by the community and run for the community. We exist to ensure that everyone, regardless of background, culture, faith, or immigration status has access to knowledge, tools, and support to plan for the future with dignity.

Our work sits at the intersection of estate planning, advance care planning, and health equity. We bring these elements together through culturally competent, trust-based engagement, particularly for migrant, diaspora, and marginalised communities.

Why We Exist

Too many families experience unnecessary distress because planning conversations never happened. Within Black, migrant, and disadvantaged communities, barriers like cultural taboos and legal complexity often prevent people from preparing adequately.

What We Do

  • Estate Planning Education: Awareness about wills and cross-border estates (UK, Africa, Asia, Caribbean).
  • Advance Care Planning (ACP): Documenting wishes for care during illness.
  • Community Engagement: Workshops with faith organisations and charities.
  • Health Equity: Addressing disproportionate impacts on marginalised communities.

Our Partners

We work with Sue Ryder, Utulivu, Project Salama, ACRE, and others committed to reducing inequality.

Our Mission

The Mission

To empower marginalised and migrant communities to plan for the future with dignity, clarity, and confidence. We increase access to legacy education, ensuring families are supported to protect their wellbeing and generational security.

Our Vision

A society where no family is left vulnerable because planning conversations never happened. We envision a world where care wishes are respected and wealth inequality gaps are closed.

Our Values

Community-Led
