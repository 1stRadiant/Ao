About Us

Angels Outreach Community Interest Company is a community-led social enterprise, created by the community and run for the community. We exist to ensure that everyone, regardless of background, culture, faith, or immigration status has access to the knowledge, tools, and support needed to plan for the future with dignity and confidence.

Our work sits at the intersection of estate planning, advance care planning, and health equity. We bring these elements together through culturally competent, trust-based engagement, particularly for migrant, diaspora, and marginalised communities.

Why We Exist

Too many families experience unnecessary distress, conflict, and financial hardship because planning conversations never happened, or happened too late. Within Black, migrant, and disadvantaged communities, barriers such as cultural taboos, lack of access to trusted information, language differences, and cross-border legal complexity often prevent people from preparing adequately.

We exist to remove those barriers. We empower families to decide how they wish to be cared for, how assets should be managed, and how wishes can be respected across different legal systems.

What We Do

  • Estate Planning Education: Awareness about wills and cross-border legacy planning (UK, Africa, Asia, and Caribbean).
  • Advance Care Planning (ACP): Documenting wishes for care and treatment during serious illness.
  • Community Engagement: Partnerships with community groups, faith organisations, and charities.
  • Health Equity: Support for marginalised families to build intergenerational resilience.

Our Partners

We collaborate with Sue Ryder, Utulivu, Project Salama, ACRE (Action for Communities in Rural England), and more.

Our Mission

The Mission

To empower marginalised and migrant communities to plan for the future with dignity, clarity, and confidence. We increase access to advance care planning, estate planning, and cross-border legacy education, ensuring generational security.

Our Vision

A society where no family is left vulnerable because planning conversations never happened. We envision communities where cultural background is never a barrier to planning ahead and care wishes are respected across all health systems.

Organ Donation

In May 2020, the new opt-out organ donation law was passed in England. This groundbreaking change empowers individuals to make an active choice, fostering a culture of awareness. Become part of a movement that values informed decision-making.

Advance Care Planning

Advance Statements and Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) ensure that your wishes are honored even if you cannot speak for yourself. We help you preserve autonomy and relieve the burden on your loved ones.

Estate Planning

Manage your affairs during your lifetime and ensure a smooth transition of assets. We cover Wills, Trusts, Beneficiary Designations, and cross-border gift planning.
