About Us

Angels Outreach CIC is a community-led social enterprise, created for the community. We ensure that everyone, regardless of background, culture, or faith, has access to the knowledge and tools needed to plan for the future with dignity.

Why We Exist

Too many families experience distress because planning conversations happened too late. We remove barriers such as cultural taboos and lack of access to information to ensure families are empowered to decide their own future care and legacy.

Our Mission

To empower marginalised and migrant communities to plan for the future with dignity and clarity. We increase access to legacy education to protect family wellbeing across borders.

Organ Donation

In May 2020, the new opt-out organ donation law was passed in England. We help educate people about their choices regarding organ donation.

Ensuring your healthcare decisions are documented and shared through Advance Statements and Lasting Power of Attorney.
